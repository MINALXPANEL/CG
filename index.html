Introduction

eFootball is a football simulation video game developed by Konami. It is the modern version of the Pro Evolution Soccer (PES) series and focuses on realistic football gameplay.

Game Information

Game Name: eFootball

Developer: Konami

Genre: Sports (Football Simulation)

Platforms: PC, PlayStation, Xbox, Mobile

Timeline and Evolution of the Game

The football game series started as Pro Evolution Soccer (PES). Over the years, many versions were released with improved gameplay and graphics. In 2021, the game was renamed as eFootball.
