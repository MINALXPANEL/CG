Introduction

eFootball is a football simulation video game developed by Konami. It is the modern version of the popular Pro Evolution Soccer (PES) series and focuses on realistic football gameplay.

Game Information

Game Name: eFootball

Developer: Konami

Genre: Sports (Football Simulation)

Available On: PC, PlayStation, Xbox and Mobile

Timeline and Evolution of the Game

The game series started as Pro Evolution Soccer (PES). Over the years, many versions were released with improved graphics and gameplay. In 2021, Konami renamed PES to eFootball and introduced a free-to-play model.
